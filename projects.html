Accessibility guide for HEIW

The accessibility guide for HEIW contains a list of guides and tools for people to follow while creating digital content such as web pages and media files.

The most interesting part of it is the "simulations" section that contains a range of simulations that emulate the type of user experiences based on different grades of disabilities such as, mobility issues, visual impairments and cognitive disabilities.
